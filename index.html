Precision Measurement.

Current transformers and voltage transformers built for accuracy at scale. Our instrument transformers enable reliable metering, protection, and control across high-voltage networks — ensuring operators maintain complete visibility over every watt flowing through the grid.

Heavy-Duty Cabling.

From high-voltage power cables to armoured conductors, we source and supply the full spectrum of industrial-grade cabling. Multi-core, XLPE insulated, and rated for the harshest conditions — delivering reliable power transmission from generation point to final distribution.
